THIS WEBSITE DEALS WITH ART PUBLISHING AS POLITICAL PLATFORM

The publication is the artwork.

I am attracted to the publication as form (or many forms) e.g., the sequences of pages, privacy of viewing.

I am attracted to the interconnections surrounding self publishing, distribution, community, and the many contexts the publication can find itself in.

I can make something cheaply, distribute it, and move on to the next idea or publication at speed.

Aara MacKillop, “the publication is the artwork.,” in Publishing Manifestos (beta version) (Berlin: Miss Read, 2018), 199.
